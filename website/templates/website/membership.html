Fort Kent Cinema Memberships

Support your local cinema. Save every time you visit.

Fort Kent Cinema is independently owned, locally run, and built for the St. John Valley community. Memberships help us keep the projectors rolling, bring in special events, and offer a cozy place to watch great films close to home.

Member Benefits at a Glance

All membership tiers include core savings plus the satisfaction of directly supporting a small-town theater.

Discounted Tickets

Enjoy member pricing on regular showings: $8 General Admission (reg. $12), $6 Matinee (reg. $8), and special member rates on double features & events.

Popcorn Perks

Save on every kernel: Small $3.25, Medium $3.50, Large $4.75, Jumbo $6.50, Big Ol’ Bag $8.00 — plus free refills on Jumbo size.

Support Local Cinema

Every membership helps us book new titles, host special screenings, and keep an independent cinema alive in Fort Kent.

Early Announcements

Be the first to hear about special events, limited screenings, and community film nights.

Member-Only Offers

Occasional member-only deals on concessions, merch, and select screenings throughout the year.

Community First

Your membership helps keep movie-going accessible, close to home, and welcoming for everyone.

How it works

1. Purchase your membership online or at the box office.

2. We’ll attach it to your name (and household, for family tiers).

3. When you buy tickets or concessions, just let us know you’re a member to receive your discounts.

FAQ

Is it worth it? If you come to the movies regularly, the savings add up quickly while helping keep Fort Kent Cinema open and thriving.

Is it recurring? Memberships are currently annual; we’ll remind you when it’s time to renew.

Questions? Reach out via our contact form or ask at the box office.
